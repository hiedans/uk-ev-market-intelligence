# UK Automotive Market Trends Analysis

## Objective

Analyse the UK automotive market and identify emerging trends related to:

- Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV)
- Plug-in Hybrid Vehicles (PHEV)
- Hybrid Vehicles (HEV)
- Internal Combustion Engine Vehicles (ICE)

## Business Questions

1. How is the UK automotive market evolving?
2. What is the impact of the ZEV Mandate?
3. Which propulsion types are growing?
4. Which propulsion types are declining?
5. What opportunities and risks exist for manufacturers?

## Planned Analysis

- Market size evolution
- Propulsion mix evolution
- BEV adoption trend
- ICE decline trend
- Competitive landscape
- Chinese brand growth
- Forecast analysis
